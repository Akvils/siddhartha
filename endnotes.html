Endnote

Siddhartha came to me during the rains of 2013. It was when the world as I knew it had been destroyed and I was lost . Mehul Jain gave me the book. His comments were that its a small book which I can read in half a day, I think he wanted me to have this perspective which he didn't share in words. I spent a week with it. I used to walk to lake early morning and sit on a bench reading it till sunset. It was a very slow week and I had just shaved my body that week. While reading it rained, I got wet and dried and I took in every sentence of the book in presence. Siddhartha's journey provided me with the space to understand that I must allow my journey to progress without my intervention. I consider it to be one of those gifts which came to me when it was needed. And now I am sharing it with you.
